System Rules & Information

📋 Shift Pattern Rules

  • Base Pattern: Day → Night → Off → Off (repeating)
  • Pulled after 1st Off: Day → Night → Night(OT) → Off → Day
  • Pulled after 2nd Off: Day → Night → Off → Day(OT) → Day
  • 8 people required in Day shift, 8 people required in Night shift

🔄 Replacement Rules

  • Only employees who had Day shift or Off on previous day can be pulled
  • Employees who had Night shift previous day CANNOT be pulled
  • Pulled employees receive overtime pay or compensatory off
  • System automatically finds best replacement based on recent pull history

📝 Leave Application Rules

  • Planned Leave: Apply at least 7 days in advance
  • Sick Leave: Emergency leave with automatic replacement
  • Emergency Leave: Immediate processing with automatic replacement
  • Priority System: Based on leave history and application time
  • Admin approval required for all leave types

👥 User Roles

  • Admin: Can view, edit roster, approve/reject leaves, make manual changes
  • User: Can view roster and apply for leaves
